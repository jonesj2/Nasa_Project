The purpose of this website is to consolidate mars weather data from NASA's InSight initiative API into a legible format. All information is provided by a JSON stream and includes temperature, wind, and air pressure measurements from the surface of Mars. Information is collected by solar day and organized accordingly.

Created by Jeylan Jones in 2022
